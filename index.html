About DJ Dorian

The Maestro Behind the Music

DJ Dorian — the force behind DJC Event Pro — is Southern California's premier event DJ with over a decade of experience turning ordinary gatherings into extraordinary memories. From intimate private parties to grand ballroom weddings and high-profile corporate galas, Dorian reads every room and delivers a custom-crafted sonic experience that keeps your guests on the dance floor all night long.

Known for his seamless blending, impeccable timing, and professional presence, DJ Dorian brings passion, precision, and an unmatched musical library to every event he touches.
